FAQ

How do I generate my PIN?
Open My Accounts and use Generate PIN after approval.
How do I check my balance?
Dashboard and account cards show the latest fetched balance.
How do I make a payment?
Open Payments, select an account, enter amount and method.
How do I view transactions?
Open Transactions to see account history and statuses.
